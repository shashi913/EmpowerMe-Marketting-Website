What is Our Aim?

Our aim is to empower individuals affected by domestic violence by providing them with a user-friendly and discreet evidence-gathering app. We strive to offer a safe and confidential platform where users can document incidents of abuse, access resources, and ultimately take steps towards seeking help and justice. Through our app, we aim to break the cycle of silence and provide support to those in need, fostering a community of empowerment, awareness, and advocacy against domestic violence."
